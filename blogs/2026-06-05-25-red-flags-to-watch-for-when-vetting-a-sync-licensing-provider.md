This Soundstripe article outlines 25 warning signs to evaluate when selecting a sync licensing provider, organized into six categories covering licensing, quality, legal, service, business, and discovery issues. The article advises that red flags in licensing or legal terms are rarely negotiable and warrant walking away, while quality and service issues may be fixable depending on vendor commitment.
